Name and Picture How is it used How it works
3-pin header Use it by connecting it to a 4-pin header Functions as connectors and recipients for jumpers
Whisker wire Use it by connecting it to the ground. It works as a switch that closes when it comes into contact with the 3-pin header.
Phototransistor The current flowing through phototransistors changes based on the amount of visible light that is present. It works by detecting variations in visible light.
Infrared detector It's used as a detector for infrared radiation. It works by sending out a low frequency beam of non-visible light and then detecting the light that gets reflected back.
Push-button (extra) Its used as a current controller It works by allowing current to pass when you push it down and block electricity when you let it go.
Capacitor (extra) Stores electrical energy in an electric field It takes energy from a battery or some other power source to move electrons to one of the plates and away from the other.
Infrared LED (extra) Used as a transmitting elements in remote-control circuits Sends out light with longer wavelengths than visible light
